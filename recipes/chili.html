Sunday Chili

Description

This Chili will take you back to the Sunday football game with your dad's chili that went well with chips, bread, cornbread, anything.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans kidney beans
  • 1 can chili beans
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 sweet onion, chopped
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound ground sausage
  • 1 pinch ground cumin, or more to taste
  • 1 pinch garlic salt, or more to taste
  • 1 pinch chili powder, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Combine kidney beans, chili beans, diced tomatoes, onion, and tomato paste in a large stockpot; cook and stir over low heat until onion is tender, about 10 minutes.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef and sausage in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease.
  3. Mix beef and sausage into bean mixture; season with cumin, garlic salt, and chili powder, Reduce heat to medium low, cover stockpot, and cook chili for 30 minutes more.
